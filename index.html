Lynettens Bådservice

At Lynettens Bådservice, our story began in the spring of 1978, coinciding with the birth of Margretheholm Harbor. To truly understand our journey, you need to know the fascinating history of the harbor's creation.

A Harbor Takes Shape

Faced with increasing waitlists for boat moorings, a group of sailors embarked on a quest to explore the possibility of a new marina. The initial option was Lynette Harbor, but that opportunity suddenly vanished.

Instead, we stumbled upon an untapped water area nestled between a former shipyard and Amager Power Plant. It was no easy feat, but we embraced the challenge. We set out to build Denmark's largest self-built project from scratch, financing it ourselves and investing thousands of hours.

From Shop to Showroom

In 1977, we were invited to establish a store at Sejlklubben Lynetten. We leased land, constructed our shop, and installed a fuel station. We expanded multiple times and became a trusted dealer of inflatable boats.

New Horizons

In 1988, we connected with South Korean shipping professionals and began importing inflatable boats under the DACON brand. We experienced success but eventually halted production.

This led us to new opportunities as distributors of marine products, including electronic nautical charts and chart plotters from NAVIONIC's. We advocated tirelessly to convince sailors of the advantages of chart plotters, and today, they are considered indispensable.

What Lies Ahead

Today, we continue to provide exciting products from abroad to the Danish market. With our technical expertise and passion, we remain open to developing new products in the future.

We value the close relationships we've built with our customers and within Sejlklubben Lynetten over the years.

Come and explore our webshop, and join us on our maritime journey!
